How to play

  1. Click Start to begin the game.
  2. For each question you will see a car logo displayed on the left-hand side.
  3. Have a look at the logo and quess what car brand is this logo for.
  4. Choose your answer by clicking on one of the three buttons on the right-hand side.
  5. A message will pop up to let you know if the answer is correct or not.
  6. Your score will be updated and the next question will come up.
  7. Don't worry if you don't get the right answer from the first time!
  8. You can try again by clicking Restart button at the end of the game.
  9. Click on X in the top-right corner of this message to return to the main page.
  10. Have fun!
